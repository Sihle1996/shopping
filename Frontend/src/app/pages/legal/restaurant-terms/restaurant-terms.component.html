Restaurant Partner Agreement

Last updated: 6 June 2026

1. Overview

This Restaurant Partner Agreement ("Agreement") governs the relationship between CraveIt (Pty) Ltd ("CraveIt") and the restaurant, food establishment, or business ("Partner") that registers on the CraveIt platform ("Platform"). By completing registration, the Partner agrees to the terms set out below.

2. Platform Services

CraveIt provides the Partner with:

  • A branded online storefront accessible via a unique URL.
  • Order management and real-time notification tools.
  • Payment processing via PayFast (card, EFT, SnapScan).
  • Delivery management with driver assignment and tracking.
  • Analytics and reporting on sales and customer activity.

3. Commission & Fees

  • CraveIt charges a platform commission on each completed order. The commission rate depends on the Partner's subscription plan (BASIC: 4%, PRO: 3%, ENTERPRISE: 2%).
  • PayFast gateway fees are charged separately by PayFast and are not included in the platform commission.
  • Subscription fees (if applicable) are billed monthly. A 14-day free trial is provided upon registration.

4. Partner Obligations

  • Maintain accurate and up-to-date menu information, including prices, descriptions, and availability.
  • Respond to incoming orders promptly. Orders not accepted within 5 minutes may be automatically cancelled.
  • Ensure all food products comply with South African health, safety, and hygiene regulations.
  • Not list prohibited items including alcohol (without a valid liquor licence), controlled substances, or counterfeit goods.
  • Maintain a valid business registration or trading licence where required by law.

5. Payouts

  • Payouts are processed on a weekly basis to the bank account registered by the Partner.
  • The payout amount equals total order revenue minus platform commission and any applicable refunds.
  • The Partner must provide accurate banking details. CraveIt is not liable for funds sent to incorrect accounts.
  • Payout records are available in the Partner's admin dashboard.

6. Refunds & Disputes

  • Customer complaints about food quality, incorrect orders, or missing items are the Partner's responsibility to resolve.
  • CraveIt may issue refunds on the Partner's behalf in cases of non-delivery or unresponsive Partners. Such refunds will be deducted from the Partner's payout.
  • Partners may dispute deductions within 7 business days by contacting CraveIt support.

7. Suspension & Termination

  • CraveIt may suspend a Partner's store for repeated customer complaints, health violations, or breach of these terms.
  • Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days' written notice.
  • Upon termination, outstanding payouts will be processed within 14 business days, less any pending refunds or disputes.
  • Trial accounts that are not upgraded after the trial period will be suspended automatically.

8. Data & Privacy

  • Customer personal information shared with Partners (name, address, phone) must be used solely for order fulfilment.
  • Partners must not use customer data for independent marketing without the customer's explicit consent.
  • Both parties must comply with POPIA. See our Privacy Policy for details.

9. Intellectual Property

Partners retain ownership of their brand, logo, and menu content. By uploading content to the Platform, Partners grant CraveIt a non-exclusive licence to display that content for the purpose of operating the Platform.

10. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the Republic of South Africa. Disputes shall be resolved in the courts of the Gauteng Division, Johannesburg.

11. Contact

For questions about this Agreement, contact us at partners@crave-it.co.za.
